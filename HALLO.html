I’ve been meaning to say this for a long time, and I guess there’s no perfect moment, so I’m just saying it now.

I like you.

I’ve liked you since Grade 9, back in speech choir. I didn’t say anything back then, I mean... I didn't even say anything until now, but I’ve liked you ever since. The more time passed, the more I realized that I care about you in a way I can’t just ignore. Being around you, talking to you, it’s been stuck with me all these time. Every little thing you did, every time we talked, every moment that shouldn’t have mattered, but did... you became part of my days in a way I couldn’t ignore.
I’ve held this in for so long because I was scared. Scared that once I say it, everything changes. Scared that I’d ruin what we have. But staying quiet has been heavier than I thought it would ever be. It’s like carrying something... something I shouldn't have been holding onto.
The flowers I gave you weren’t just flowers. They were my words before I could actually say them. They were my way of showing you, without saying the words out loud, how much you mean to me. But it’s still not enough. Nothing is enough until I tell you myself. I like you, not because of a single moment or because people noticed. Not because of jokes or teasing or random things that happened. I like you because of you. Because of the way you are, the way you laugh, the way you can change my day without even knowing it. Because being around you just feels different.
I don’t expect anything from you. I’m not asking you to feel the same or to say anything back. I just needed to finally say it. You deserve to hear it from me, not from anyone else, not from hints or jokes or the teasing. I like you. I’ve liked you all this time. And even if nothing changes, I needed to be honest.

Neil
